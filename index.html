Commission Structure

Scots Law Connect operates on the following commission tiers:

  • Standard Tier (17.5%): Basic listing, up to 10 consultations/month
  • Professional Tier (22.5%): Featured listing, unlimited consultations
  • Premium Tier (27.5%): Premium placement, dedicated support

Payment Terms

Payments are processed within 15-30 days of service completion, depending on your selected tier. You are responsible for setting your consultation fees within our recommended pricing guidelines.

Professional Requirements

You must maintain a valid practicing certificate from the Law Society of Scotland and adequate professional indemnity insurance. All advice provided must comply with Scottish legal standards and professional conduct rules.
